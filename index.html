Smart irigation with moisture -detective sensor

The project is subjected to the increased hindrance in watering the plants on dry days as we can’t always keep an eye on the soil’s moisture level.Detecting sensor we used will keep a check on the water content(moisture level) and accordingly pump the water from nearby source. The sensor will check.Or the set range of moist surrounding soil from the “aurduino UNO”. which has the coding according to the limit set.It will automatically increase and decrease the pouring as per the needs. the power will be supplied by Power Bank and can be altered as per requirements(whether large scale or small scale).

Team Members: Ashwin sharma - XII "C", Tanishka lovevanshi - XII "C", Avni rawat - IX "A", Sanskriti verma - IX "A"

The project is made to Spread awareness to Save trees save paper.This project will help us passing any message almost immediately without any delay just by sending a SMS which is better and more reliable than the old traditional way of passing the message.
